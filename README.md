The Indian aviation sector plays a vital role in economic development, tourism, and logistics.

---

<h2><a class="anchor" id="problem-statement"></a>Problem Statement</h2>

The aviation industry experienced:

- A major decline in 2020 due to COVID-19  
- Rapid recovery post-2021  
- Regional performance differences  

There was a need for a clear and structured visualization to track performance, recovery trends, and growth metrics effectively.

This dashboard solves that problem by presenting KPI-driven insights in a single interactive view.

<h2><a class="anchor" id="result"></a>Results & Conclusion</a></h2>

- 2020 shows a significant drop due to pandemic impact  
- Strong recovery trend visible after 2021  
- Southern region leads in aircraft movement  
- Delhi (NCT) shows highest domestic operations  
- Cargo remained comparatively stable during disruption  

**Final Insight:**  
The Indian aviation sector demonstrates resilience and structured recovery, with clear long-term growth trends visible through data-driven analysis.
